MOONLIGHT MUSIC SCHOOL

At Moonlight Music School in Charlotte, NC, we offer more than just private lessons in piano, violin, flute, and voice. We provide a nurturing environment where your child can grow, finding joy and pride in every note and song. From the very first lesson, students dive into music—playing right away while exploring notation, theory, and history. As they progress, their creativity blossoms through improvisation, understanding that music is a journey of the heart and mind.

Piano

Our piano lessons for kids focus on developing a strong foundation in music theory, hand coordination, and rhythm. Through a combination of fun and structured lessons, students will learn to read music, play simple songs, and build confidence as they progress.

Flute

Students will learn proper flute technique, breathing exercises, and note reading, all while playing simple and enjoyable songs. These lessons foster creativity, concentration, and teamwork, as children gain skills that allow them to perform both solo and in groups.

Violin

Students will start by developing good posture and basic techniques, including bowing and finger placement. Through interactive and engaging lessons, kids will gradually learn to play beautiful melodies and classical pieces, enhancing their musical ear, coordination, and focus.

Voice

Through fun warm-ups, vocal exercises, and age-appropriate songs, students will improve their pitch, tone, and breath control. These lessons build confidence and stage presence while promoting healthy vocal habits and self-expression.
